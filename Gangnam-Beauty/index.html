Gangnam Beauty
Gangnam Beauty is a thought-provoking visual investigation into the contradictions of South Korea’s beauty standards. Named after the term that critiques those who undergo cosmetic procedures to fit rigid ideals, this project examines how the pursuit of perfection can lead to both admiration and harsh societal judgment. Through the lens of a criminal investigation, Gangnam Beauty challenges viewers to question how beauty is defined, who gets to define it, and what it costs to conform or resist those expectations.
Publication
The book component of Gangnam Beauty offers critical depth through a variety of research methods, including historical research, media analyses, personal reflections, and survey results. The publication unpacks how beauty standards are enforced and judged, revealing the societal pressure, contradictions, and emotional toll behind the term Gangnam Beauty.
Posters
The poster series introduces the key themes of Gangnam Beauty through rhetorical questions that invite self-reflection. Each poster is designed to feel interrogative—directed at the viewer—mirroring the scrutiny individuals face under rigid beauty standards.

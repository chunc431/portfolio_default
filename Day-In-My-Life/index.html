A Day In My Life
“A Day In My Life” is a rotoscope animation project that transforms a typical day into an engaging visual narrative. This vlog-style animation uses neon colors drawn over a gray background to highlight the joy in daily routines. The abstract line-work, contrasting colors, and rhythmic patterns focus attention on key elements of each scene, turning ordinary moments into an extraordinary experience. This project showcases the power of animation to reimagine the mundane, inviting viewers to see the everyday world in a vibrant new light.

This project combines a supportive publication, interactive activities, and engaging posters to address teenage stress. Its core message of solidarity reassures teenagers they're not alone, normalizing stress discussions and promoting a community of support and understanding.
Publication
This book is an engaging mental wellness guide that combines informative content about the psychology of stress with interactive activities to help manage it. Through simplifying complex ideas into relatable concepts, teenagers are able to understand and tackle their stress more effectively.
Posters
The motion graphic posters animate four key stress-related phrases: "Overloaded," "Drained," "Lonely," and "Defeated." Each poster uniquely visualizes these stages of stress, offering a dynamic representation of the emotional journey teenagers often experience.

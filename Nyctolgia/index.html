Nyctolgia
The Nyctolgia branding project celebrates New York City’s iconic neighborhoods through a cocktail bar experience that blends nostalgia with modern mixology. Each drink is crafted to represent the history, culture, and atmosphere of a particular NYC area. The project aims to create an immersive and educational experience that guides audiences through design and storytelling.

Menu
The printed menu highlights Nyctolgia’s bold branding, showcasing drink ingredients and their NYC neighborhood inspirations with vibrant visuals and historical insights.
Poster
The motion poster promotes Nyctolgia through dynamic visuals, blending NYC-inspired mixology with nostalgia for social media, in-bar displays, and campaigns.
Website
The Nyctolgia website immerses users in NYC-inspired cocktails, featuring detailed drink histories, neighborhood visuals, and interactive features like memory submissions. It also includes bar essentials like an online menu, event details, and reservations.

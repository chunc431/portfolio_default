Rodent Inspector
"Rodent Inspector" is an interactive, data-driven website that leverages NYC Open Data to map rodent sightings across New York City. Designed to resemble an inspector's report, it offers a unique and engaging way to visualize and learn about rodent activity in the city. By utilizing JSON data to plot sightings on a map, this innovative tool serves as a valuable resource for enhancing public safety and awareness in NYC.

Kellogg's Brand Redesign
This proposal outlines a comprehensive reactivation of the Kellogg's brand, encompassing a new logo, innovative packaging design, and designs for promotional items. The focus of this redesign is to appeal to Kellogg's target audience: families with children and health-conscious individuals. The project aims to create a fresh, family-friendly, and health-oriented brand image, aligning Kellogg's visual identity with the evolving preferences of its consumers.

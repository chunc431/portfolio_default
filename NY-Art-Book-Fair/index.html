NY Art Book Fair
This project showcases a vibrant series of typographic designs for promotional materials, including a poster, a social media campaign, flyers, and pamphlets, complemented by a digital website. Inspired by Printed Matter's iconic posters for the NY Art Book Fair, the designs feature bright colors and innovative typographic play, blending modern flair with classic artistry.

Vintique
New York Fashion Week (NYFW) is a highlight of the fashion industry, yet breaking into this glamorous world can be daunting for newcomers. This project addresses this challenge by providing clear, accessible insights into NYFW trends, advice from past interns, and seasonal archives.
Publication
Vintique is a dynamic zine that delves into New York Fashion Week, featuring the latest trends, insightful interviews with interns, and a look back at iconic past season looks. Designed as a guide for those aspiring to join the fashion industry, it bridges historical context with contemporary insights, offering readers a unique perspective on the evolution and inner workings of New York Fashion Week.
